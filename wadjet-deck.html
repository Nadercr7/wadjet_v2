What does the Eye of Horus symbolize?
𓁟 Thoth
The Wedjat Eye (𓂀) represents healing, protection, and royal power. Each part corresponds to a fraction (1/2, 1/4, 1/8...) used in ancient Egyptian mathematics. Together they represent wholeness — the restoration of Horus's eye after his battle with Set. 𓂧
